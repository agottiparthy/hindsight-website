## Overview

LaunchDarkly, a leading feature management platform, needed a more scalable and actionable way to gather competitive intelligence. With 200+ sellers in market and 25+ competitors to track, their manual processes — digging through Slack, ad-hoc rep conversations, and basic web searches — left gaps in speed, consistency, and impact.

Tye Davis, Sr. Manager of Competitive Intelligence, turned to Hindsight. What started as a search for external monitoring quickly became a transformation of LaunchDarkly’s competitive program.

> “At LaunchDarkly, we had a very manual process… It was coming from Slack, from conversations with reps, or just going to competitor websites. It was tedious.”

## The Challenge

> “We had one poor rep per month present why they lost a deal. Just one deal a month. With Hindsight, we’ve 200x’d that.”

- Competitive intelligence relied on manual monitoring (Slack, rep anecdotes, watching calls, web browsing)
- Monthly “loss review” meetings with a single rep gave limited, lagging insight
- CI content (battlecards, messaging, product comparisons) was hard to keep fresh

With 200+ reps, scaling competitive support was nearly impossible for a one-person CI team.

## The Solution

1. **Comprehensive Monitoring:** Automated competitor tracking across web, product updates, and reviews
2. **Win-Loss Intelligence:** Automatic seller surveys after every closed deal, multiplying feedback volume 100–200x
3. **Deal Analysis:** AI-driven tagging of win/loss drivers, competitor mentions, and product gaps from Salesforce, Gong, and deal notes
4. **Real-Time Enablement:** Daily insights turned into one-pagers, decks, and competitive content for reps
5. **AI-Powered Roleplay:** Integrated CI data into enablement training, helping reps practice objection handling and competitor positioning

> “With Hindsight, I’ve been able to create fresh one-pagers and customer-facing decks daily. Even if a rep asks, ‘How do we integrate with Shopify?’ I can get them a tailored asset right away.”

## Results

- **95% Faster Time to Insight:** Reps get competitive answers in minutes, not days
- **200+ Reps Supported:** A one-person CI team now scales to the entire field
- **25+ Competitors Tracked** with fresh, detailed intelligence
- **Real-Time, Trustworthy Data:** Reps enter deals with confidence, knowing competitor info is current
- **Strategic Resource:** CI now informs leadership, product, and sales teams with daily win-loss and feature gap insights

> “I now have very crisp, up-to-date competitive intelligence every single day. This isn’t quarterly or yearly — it’s real-time.”

## Key Takeaway

Hindsight enabled LaunchDarkly to replace manual, lagging processes with a scalable, AI-driven CI and win-loss program — supporting hundreds of reps with real-time insights and actionable content, while turning a one-person CI function into a company-wide competitive advantage.

> “My sales reps are not going into deals with outdated information anymore. They can compete with confidence.”
